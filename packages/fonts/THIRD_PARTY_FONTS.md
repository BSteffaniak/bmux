# Third-party bundled fonts

This crate can optionally embed third-party font files for high-fidelity recording exports.

## Ghostty Nerd preset

When `bundled-ghostty-fonts` is enabled, the crate embeds:

- `JetBrainsMonoNerdFont-Regular.ttf`
- `JetBrainsMonoNerdFont-Bold.ttf`
- `JetBrainsMonoNerdFont-Italic.ttf`
- `JetBrainsMonoNerdFont-BoldItalic.ttf`

Source: Ghostty repository `src/font/res/`

- https://github.com/ghostty-org/ghostty

Licenses and attribution are provided in `packages/fonts/licenses/OFL.txt` and
`packages/fonts/licenses/GHOSTTY_FONT_README.md`.
